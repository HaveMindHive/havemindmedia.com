What This Shows

In the Epoch Framework, YOU are always at the center of your coordinate system. The two boundaries — consciousness (at 729) and physical (at 1000) — are always measured relative to YOUR position.

Move the slider and watch how the distances change. When you move toward one boundary, it gets closer while the other moves further away. There's no "correct" position — only your position.

The key insight: Every observer creates their own map. The geometry is the same, but the view depends on where you stand.
