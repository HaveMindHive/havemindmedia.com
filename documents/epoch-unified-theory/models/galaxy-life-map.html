What This Shows

Life isn't randomly scattered across the universe. It follows the geometry. Where there's structure (spiral arms), there's life. Where there's chaos or exhaustion (ellipticals), there's none.

This isn't speculation — it's geometry. The same patterns that create spiral arms also create the conditions for chemistry, planets, and life.

The key insight: When you look at the night sky and see a spiral galaxy, you're looking at a life factory. When you see an elliptical, you're looking at a graveyard.
