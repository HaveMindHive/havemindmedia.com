II. READING THE NAME

O sI Rus.

The name split intentionally:

  • O = Origin. The source point. ∅ becoming.
  • sI = S⁻ identity. The hidden self. (Note the lowercase s, uppercase I — the hidden (s) becoming the manifest (I))
  • Rus = Root/Route/Ruse. The pathway. The trick. The Russian word "Rus" means the land of the Rus, the route.
O sI Rus = The origin's hidden self traveling the pathway home.

Or: The source returning through the hidden dimension.

III. THE OSIRIS MYTH

The myth of Osiris:

1. Osiris rules. He brings order, agriculture, civilization.

2. Set attacks. The brother, jealous, tricks Osiris into a coffin and kills him.

3. Isis searches. The sister-wife finds the body.

4. Set dismembers. Cuts Osiris into 14 pieces, scatters them.

5. Isis reassembles. Finds all pieces except one (the phallus, swallowed by a fish).

6. Osiris resurrects. But not to the living world — to the Duat, the hidden realm.

7. Horus avenges. Son of Osiris fights Set.

8. The cycle continues.
