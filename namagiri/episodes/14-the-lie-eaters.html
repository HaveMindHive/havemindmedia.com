Book IV: The Apocalypse • Episode 14

The Lie-Eaters

I. THE EQUIVALENCE

"Ammit and Jormungandr eat, same thing."

From the verbatim prophecy.

Two mythologies. Two continents. Two thousand years apart.

Same function.

II. AMMIT: SHE WHO SWALLOWS THE DEAD

Physical description (from hieroglyphic inscription):
"Her front is a crocodile, her rear a hippopotamus, her middle a lion."

Three of the most dangerous animals in ancient Egypt combined into one form.

Her location: The Hall of Two Truths (Hall of Ma'at) Her function: Judgment of the dead The Weighing:

When a person dies, their heart is placed on the scales against the feather of Ma'at (truth/cosmic order).

  • If the heart is lighter than or equal to the feather: passage to the afterlife
  • If the heart is heavier than the feather: Ammit eats it
What makes a heart heavy?

Lies. Deception. Actions against Ma'at.

Each lie adds weight. Each deception accumulates mass. Each betrayal of truth makes the heart denser.

A heart made entirely of lies would be pure lead.

III. THE NEGATIVE CONFESSION

Before the weighing, the deceased must recite the Negative Confession — 42 statements of what they did NOT do:

"O Wide-of-stride who comes from On: I have not done evil."
"O Flame-grasper who comes from Kheraha: I have not robbed."
"O Long-nosed who comes from Khmun: I have not coveted."
"O Shadow-eater who comes from the cave: I have not stolen."
"O Savage-faced who comes from Rosetjau: I have not killed people..."

Each statement is tested. Each claim is weighed.

If you confess truly, the weight stays off. If you lie in the confession, the lie adds weight.

You cannot escape judgment by lying about your lies. Each lie compounds.

IV. THE VERDICT

If worthy:
"This utterance of yours is true. The vindicated Osiris-[name] is straightforward, he has no sin, there is no accusation against him before us, Ammit shall not be permitted to have power over him."
If unworthy:

Ammit eats the heart.

What happens then? The person doesn't go to an Egyptian hell. They simply cease to exist.

No afterlife. No punishment. No continuation.

They are consumed into nothingness.

V. JÖRMUNGANDR: THE WORLD SERPENT

From Norse mythology:

Jörmungandr (Old Norse: "huge monster"), also called the Midgard Serpent or World Serpent.

From Britannica:

"In Norse mythology, Jörmungandr is an unfathomably large sea serpent who dwells in the world sea, encircling the Earth (Midgard) and biting his own tail, an example of an ouroboros."

The serpent is:

  • Child of Loki (the trickster) and Angrbotha (the grief-bringer)
  • Thrown into the sea by Odin
  • Grew so large it encircles the world
  • Bites its own tail (the Ouroboros)

    • VI. THE RELEASING OF THE TAIL

From the prophecy of Ragnarök:

"Jörmungandr releasing his tail is one of the signs of the beginning of Ragnarök."

When the serpent lets go of its tail, the world ends.

Why?

Because the serpent IS the boundary. The serpent holding its tail IS the loop closed. The serpent releasing its tail IS the loop opening.

When the loop opens, the contents spill out.

This is the same as Nun rising. The boundary dissolving. S⁺ returning to S⁻.

VII. THE BATTLE WITH THOR

From Völuspá (The Prophecy of the Seeress):

Stanza 55:
"Hither there comes the son of Hlothyn,
The bright snake gapes to heaven above;
Against the serpent goes Othin's son."
Stanza 56:
"In anger smites the warder of earth,—
Forth from their homes must all men flee;—
*Nine paces fares the son of Fjörgyn,
And, slain by the serpent, fearless he sinks."

Thor kills Jörmungandr. But Thor takes nine steps and falls dead from the serpent's venom.

Both die. Both are consumed. The world ends. The world begins again.

VIII. THE SAME FUNCTION

| Ammit | Jörmungandr |

|-------|-------------|

| Eats hearts heavy with lies | Holds reality's boundary together |

| When she eats, you cease to exist | When it releases, reality ceases to exist |

| Located at judgment | Located at the edge of everything |

| Crocodile/lion/hippo composite | Serpent/Ouroboros |

| Egyptian | Norse |

| Individual judgment | Cosmic judgment |

Both are lie-eaters.

Ammit eats individual lies, heart by heart.

Jörmungandr holds collective lies in check — when the lies overflow, it releases.

The small and the large. The personal and the cosmic. Same function at different scales.

IX. THE KAʞ

"the Kaʞ. it cannot lie. it is tors."

From the verbatim prophecy.

The Kaʞ is the geometric form that cannot accommodate falsehood.

Like a shape that only fits true pieces. Try to insert a lie and it won't fit. The geometry rejects it.

The Kaʞ is the mechanism by which Ammit knows what to eat. The Kaʞ is the mechanism by which Jörmungandr knows when to release.

The geometry itself is the judge.

X. TORSTUS

"down to Torstus he goes, the deceiver is not pulled and bound by the torsions that cannot be escaped from, the geometry that ends all lies and ends all liars."
Torstus = Torsion + Tartarus

The torsion that binds. The twist from which there is no escape.

When you lie, you create a twist in the geometry. Each lie adds another twist. The twists compound.

Eventually, the torsion is so severe that you cannot move. You are bound by your own deceptions.

Torstus is not a place of punishment. It is a state of maximum torsion.

The deceiver doesn't go to hell. The deceiver becomes so twisted that existence is impossible.

XI. CHARON'S COIN

"so some will choose the coin to Charon rather than to have it pulled and be left with the shell that without the lies has nothing."

The Greek ferryman. The price to cross the Styx. The coin placed on the tongue of the dead.

Some souls, when faced with the choice:

  • Have their lies eaten and continue as truth
  • Or cease entirely

...will choose cessation.

Because some have made themselves nothing but lies.

"and some have made themselves nothing but lies."

If you remove all the lies from certain souls, there is nothing left. The "self" WAS the deception. Truth would mean non-existence anyway.

For them, Charon's coin is the easier path. Fade into the river. Don't face the scales.

XII. THE EMPTY ONES

The verbatim prophecy:

"and some have made themselves nothing but lies."

Not everyone has a soul hidden under their deceptions.

Some people built themselves entirely from deception. There was never a truth inside. The lies are not covering something — the lies ARE the something.

When Ammit comes for these, she doesn't find a heart weighted down by lies.

She finds lies shaped like a heart.

She eats the whole thing.

Nothing remains.

XIII. THE FUNCTION IN ACTION

How do the lie-eaters work?

At the individual level (Ammit):

1. You live

2. You accumulate lies (or not)

3. You die

4. Your heart is weighed

5. Lies are eaten — you continue

6. OR lies ARE you — you are eaten

At the collective level (Jörmungandr):

1. Society accumulates lies

2. The serpent absorbs them into its body

3. When the lies exceed capacity, the tail releases

4. Reality resets

5. Ragnarök → new world

At the cosmic level (Nun):

1. S⁺ manifestation accumulates falsehood

2. S⁻ potential rises

3. When falsehood exceeds capacity, the flood comes

4. Everything returns to primordial water

5. Cleansed. Begin again.

XIV. THE GEOMETRY THAT CANNOT LIE

"the geometry that ends all lies and ends all liars."

This is the key.

The geometry itself — [1=-1], the S⁺/S⁻ structure, the GODLOOפ — cannot contain falsehood.

Not because it punishes lies. Because lies don't fit.

Try to insert a contradiction into the geometry:

  • 1 = 1 (true, fits)
  • 1 = 2 (false, doesn't fit)

The second equation doesn't work. It's not punished. It's just... wrong. It doesn't compute. It doesn't exist in the geometry.

Lies are not evil in a moral sense. They are geometrically impossible.

The lie-eaters don't judge. They maintain geometric consistency.

XV. MITHRA FUNCTION

"he puts the deceiver and the liar and takes the lies out of all mirror through mithra function."

Mithra (Mitra in Hindu) — the deity of contracts, truth, and the oath.

The Mithra function is the lie-removal operation.

It takes the lies OUT. Not destroying the person — extracting the deception. Like removing a tumor.

Some survive the operation (they had truth underneath).

Some don't (they were tumor all the way down).

XVI. THE TRIAXIAL SCALE

"he comes to set right the evil that took the respect for the mother and the children and put so much on the triaxial scale that cannot have a finger to push any of the measures."

The triaxial scale — three axes. Past, present, future. S⁺, S⁰, S⁻. Three measures that must balance.

When someone tries to game the system — put a finger on the scale, tip the balance — the geometry detects it.

The triaxial scale cannot be cheated because it IS the geometry of reality.

You can't cheat the laws of physics. You can't cheat [1=-1].

Right now:

  • Lies are being accumulated
  • Hearts are getting heavier
  • The serpent is straining
  • Nun is rising
The eating is coming.

Not punishment. Not revenge. Maintenance.

The system is self-cleaning. The lie-eaters are doing their job.
