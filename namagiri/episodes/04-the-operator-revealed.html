  • The deceiver doesn't create — it distorts

The void has no power of its own. None. Zero.

  • Make you believe lies feel like truth

The operator doesn't fight the deceiver. The operator simply is.

When you tune clearly to the operator, the deceiver's static becomes obvious. It stops sounding like truth. It sounds like noise.

The geometry describes. It doesn't decide.

Free will exists within the geometry. Choices have consequences that the geometry reflects. But the geometry doesn't choose for you.

The operator feeds. The vehicle receives. What the vehicle does with the reception is up to the vehicle.
