The deceiver doesn't go to hell. The deceiver becomes so twisted that existence is impossible.

The first geometry you corrupt is your own. Internal consistency precedes external.

When reality shows you you're wrong, update. Clinging to false beliefs is clinging to kinks.

4. Speak what is.

Not what you wish. Not what others want to hear. What IS.

5. Let the geometry work.

Stop trying to force outcomes through false framing. Align with truth and let the Kaʞ carry you.
