Balance Is Everywhere

Balance isn't just about seesaws. It's one of nature's deepest patterns:

  • ⚖ A bicycle stays up because forces balance
  • ⚖ A building stands because weight is evenly distributed
  • ⚖ An engine runs smoothly because vibrations cancel out
  • ⚖ Even math equations balance: what's on the left equals the right

The Circle's Secret Balance

Remember the circle? It has perfect balance built in.

If you place four points evenly around a circle — at 0°, 90°, 180°, and 270° — and draw arrows from the center to each point...

Watch the four arrows. Together, they point nowhere. They balance to zero!

Sum = (0, 0) — Perfect Balance!

When points are evenly spaced around a circle, their arrows cancel out. They sum to zero.

This is why:

  • • Car engines use 4 or 6 cylinders at even spacing — vibrations cancel
  • • Windmills have 3 blades at 120° apart — forces balance
  • • Electric motors use 3-phase power at 120° — smooth rotation

The circle creates balance naturally.
Evenly spaced points always sum to zero.
