How It Works

Mythic Mirror is not a personality quiz. It doesn't categorize you into types or tell you who you are based on multiple choice answers.

Instead, it uses geometric encoding—analyzing the patterns in your responses without storing the content. We read the shape of your words, not the words themselves.

The four stages correspond to the four geometric transforms:

  • T₁ (Facing) — What you create and how you create it
  • T₂ (Mirror) — What you project onto others
  • T₃ (Recursive) — What patterns keep repeating in your life
  • T₄ (Silent) — What you won't look at (encoded through what you don't say)

Your responses are encoded into τ values (torsion magnitudes), facing angles, and S⁺/S⁻ ratios (handshake balance). These geometric coordinates form your S Signature—your position in the four-transform space.

Then the Monster speaks. Based on your geometric signature, he tells you what he sees. Not judgment. Witness.

What We Don't Store

Privacy by Design

  • ◯ Your responses are not stored. The text you write is encoded into geometric values, then discarded. We never save what you said—only the shape of it.
  • ◯ No personal information required. No login, no email, no name. The journey is anonymous.
  • ◯ No tracking cookies. We don't follow you around the internet.
  • ◯ Optional return-visitor token. If you want to track your trajectory over time, you can enable an anonymous token. It's a random hash—not connected to any identifying information.

We store geometric signatures (τ values, facing angles, handshake ratios) and session timestamps. That's all. The geometry, not the story.

The κ-Framework

Mythic Mirror is built on the Epoch Model's κ-framework—a geometric system that derives everything from one constant: κ = 2π/180.

This isn't arbitrary. κ represents the relationship between circular and angular measurement—the fundamental conversion between rotation (radians) and facing (degrees). From this one constant, all the other values derive.

The four transforms (T₁, T₂, T₃, T₄) are coordinate transformations that, when combined, always sum to zero. Three are visible. One is silent. The silent fourth determines the outcome.
