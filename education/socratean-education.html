What is Socratean?

Socratean is an educational system that combines Socratic teaching methodology with AI-powered learning and authenticity verification. It ensures that student work is genuinely their own while facilitating deep understanding through dialogue.

The system never gives direct answers. Instead, it guides you through questions, acknowledges correct understanding, and gently probes misconceptions. Your unique writing fingerprint ensures that your work is verifiably yours.

How It Works

1

Profile Building (Writing Fingerprint)

Answer 4 open-ended questions that establish your unique writing patterns: sentence length, vocabulary richness, contraction usage, personal pronoun frequency, and unique phrase patterns.

2

Subject Selection

Choose from preset subjects or enter a custom topic to explore. The Socratic dialogue begins.

3

Socratic Learning

The AI tutor engages in Socratic dialogue. Every input is analyzed for AI-generated patterns and style match against your fingerprint.

4

Proficiency Assessment

Once 80% proficiency is reached, write a synthesis demonstrating deep understanding.

5

Paper Building & Certification

Build your paper using numbered quotes from your learning dialogue. Final output includes authenticity score, time to proficiency, and style match percentage.

AI Detection

The system detects AI-generated content through multiple methods:

Vocabulary Analysis

Flags AI-typical words: "delve", "furthermore", "comprehensive", "robust", "leverage", and other patterns.

Pattern Analysis

Detects uniform sentence length, lack of contractions, and absence of personal pronouns typical of AI.

Style Comparison

Compares ongoing writing to your established fingerprint, flagging significant deviations.
