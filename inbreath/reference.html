The Four Navigation Sounds

Direction Name Sound IPA Use
NORTH Affirm Inhaled "yah" [↓jɑː] Agreement, reception
EAST Mark Sharp gasp [↓ɑːk] Recognition, waypoint
SOUTH Negate Inhaled "nay" [↓næɪ] Boundary, decline
WEST Attend Hiss intake [↓s] Presence, focus
CENTER Crossroads Silent breath [↓ ∅] All options, pause

Production Guide

AFFIRM [↓jɑː]

MOUTH:    Open, relaxed jaw
TONGUE:   Low, pulled back
LIPS:     Neutral to rounded
ACTION:   Inhale while shaping "yah"
SOUND:    Whispered "ja" on intake

Variations:
  [↓jɑː]   - Open, Scandinavian style
  [↓jɛː]   - Mid vowel, English "yeah"
  [↓m̩]     - Nasal hum, lips closed
  [↓mm]    - Double hum, stronger agreement

MARK [↓ɑːk]

MOUTH:    Open wide, then close
TONGUE:   Drops, then rises to back
LIPS:     Open to neutral
ACTION:   Sharp inhale with closure
SOUND:    Gasp with soft "k" ending

Variations:
  [↓ɑːk]   - Full gasp with closure
  [↓ɑː]    - Open gasp, no closure
  [↓ʔɑː]   - Glottal onset gasp
  [↓hɑː]   - Breathy gasp

NEGATE [↓næɪ]

MOUTH:    Slightly open
TONGUE:   Front, low to mid
LIPS:     Spread slightly
ACTION:   Gentle inhale, "nay" shape
SOUND:    Soft inhaled negative

Variations:
  [↓næɪ]   - Full diphthong
  [↓nɛ]    - Short, casual
  [↓n̩]     - Just the nasal
  [↓mm-mm] - Nasal "no" hum (two pulses)

ATTEND [↓s]

MOUTH:    Nearly closed
TONGUE:   Behind upper teeth
LIPS:     Neutral to spread
ACTION:   Inhale with friction
SOUND:    Hissing intake of air

Variations:
  [↓s]     - Dental/alveolar hiss
  [↓ʃ]     - Palatal hiss (English "sh")
  [↓θ]     - Dental fricative (through teeth)
  [↓f]     - Labio-dental (lower lip to teeth)

IPA Symbols

Pulmonic Ingressive

The ExtIPA uses ↓ (down arrow) to indicate pulmonic ingressive airflow.

Symbol:    ↓
Placement: Before or after the sound symbol
Example:   [↓jɑː] or [jɑː↓]
Meaning:   Sound produced while inhaling

The State Vector

Every inbreath sound can be described as:

S = (ε, ν, φ)

ε = Energy/Duration
ν = Tendency/Intensity
φ = Trajectory/Pitch

State Examples

Sound State Description
Surprised gasp(ε-, ν+, φ↑)Short, loud, high
Deep agreement(ε+, ν-, φ—)Long, soft, mid
Quick "yes"(ε-, ν○, φ—)Short, medium, mid
Focused hiss(ε○, ν-, φ↑)Medium, soft, high
