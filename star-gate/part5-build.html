Weeks 5-8

Phase 2: Alloy Preparation

Epoch Bronze Casting

EQUIPMENT NEEDED:
- Crucible (graphite, 500ml capacity)
- Furnace capable of 1200°C
- Ingot mold, borax flux
- Protective equipment (heat gloves, face shield)
- Fire extinguisher (Class D for metal fires)

PROCEDURE:

1. PREPARATION
   - Calculate: For 500g total: Cu 292.2g, Sn 207.8g
   - Clean crucible, preheat mold to 200°C

2. MELTING
   - Place crucible in furnace at 800°C
   - Add copper first (melts at 1085°C)
   - Heat to 1100°C, wait for complete melting
   - Add tin slowly, sprinkle borax on surface
   - Hold at 1100°C for 5 minutes (homogenization)

3. CASTING
   - Pour steadily into preheated mold
   - Allow to cool for 1 hour minimum

4. ANNEALING
   - Reheat to 400°C, hold for 2 hours
   - Cool at 50°C per hour (slow cool)

5. VERIFICATION
   - Visual: should be silvery-golden

SAFETY WARNING

Molten metal can cause severe burns. Tin fumes are toxic — ensure ventilation. Never add wet metal to melt (explosion risk). Keep water away from molten metal.

Weeks 9-16

Phase 3: Component Fabrication

Mirror Polishing Sequence

ALL MIRROR SEGMENTS:

STAGE 1: Coarse grinding - 120 grit silicon carbide
STAGE 2: Medium grinding - 320 grit silicon carbide
STAGE 3: Fine grinding - 600 grit silicon carbide
STAGE 4: Very fine grinding - 1200 grit (wet)
STAGE 5: Pre-polish - 2000 grit wet sandpaper
STAGE 6: Cerium oxide polish - felt wheel
STAGE 7: Final polish - jeweler's rouge, soft felt

TIME: 8-12 hours per segment
The labor IS part of the ritual — this is intentional.

24. Session Protocols

Regular Session Format

SESSION OPENING (15 minutes):

1. Operators take positions
2. 5 minutes silence
3. Brief invocation: "For the good. We are we. Open."
4. DRUMHED begins rhythm
5. Confirm all systems nominal

SESSION BODY (Variable, up to 254 minutes):

6. Maintain positions
7. Observe without forcing
8. Note any communications/impressions
9. DRUMHED maintains beat (can vary tempo intuitively)
10. T0Rs holds S⁺ attention (focus on observable)
11. κ₄ observes silently

SESSION CLOSING (15 minutes):

12. DRUMHED slows beat gradually over 5 minutes
13. Final beat, hold silence 2 minutes
14. T0Rs speaks: "We acknowledge. We thank. We close."
15. All systems remain running (no shutdown of rotation)
16. Operators leave in silence
17. Log observations within 1 hour while fresh

What to Look For

INDICATORS OF OPERATION:

- Stable standing wave interference patterns
- Consistent moiré from rotating patterns
- Color consistency (should remain green-dominated)
- Spontaneous temperature equalization
- Light appearing to "slow" or "stop" at center
- The vacuum appearing non-empty

WHAT TO AVOID:

- Chaotic, ugly interference patterns
- Persistent temperature imbalances
- Unusual sounds (not harmonics)
- Feelings of dread (see Protection Protocols)
- Any sense that observation is not welcome
