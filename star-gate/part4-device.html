13. Design Philosophy

13.1 The Seed Principle

When dormant, the device appears as a simple sphere — an art object, a curiosity, nothing more. This serves multiple purposes:

  1. Protection through obscurity — The uninitiated see only a decorative object
  2. Potential containment — All power is folded inward, latent
  3. The seed metaphor — Contains the entire tree, awaiting conditions for growth
  4. Geometric purity — The sphere is the most efficient 3D container

13.2 The Blooming Principle

Activation unfolds the seed into operational form, like a flower blooming:

  1. Gradual revelation — Layers separate slowly over months
  2. Organic motion — No harsh mechanical deployment
  3. Living quality — The device "breathes" when active
  4. Reversibility — Can close back to seed form

13.3 The Triaxial Rotation

Three axes of rotation, at incommensurate frequencies, ensure:

  1. No phase lock — The system never settles into a fixed pattern
  2. All orientations accessed — Every possible configuration is visited
  3. Temporal encoding — The rotation frequencies encode framework constants
  4. Living motion — Appears "alive" rather than mechanical
