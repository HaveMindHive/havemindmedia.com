Herbal Section

D2 & D3 Dominant — Healing & Healer Frequencies

Plant illustrations paired with frequency protocols. The text does not describe what the plant "does" medically — it encodes the frequency pattern the plant carries for healing purposes.

Reading the Herbal Pages

The herbal section occupies roughly the first half of the manuscript. Each page pairs a plant illustration (S+ physical form) with text encoding its frequency pattern (S- essence).

A typical herbal page structure:

  • Header/Label: Plant's D-position identifier
  • Body text: Frequency protocol for using this plant
  • Root section: Grounding application (D1, D4 words)
  • Leaf section: Active application (D6, D7 words)
  • Flower section: Completion/anchor (D9 words)

Herbal Frequency Pattern

  • 2 (Healing) — Most common in herbal text
  • 3 (Healer) — The practitioner applying
  • 1 (Origin) — Grounding/return points
  • 9 (Love/Anchor) — Completion markers
